Good design speaks volumes

Hi there!

I'm a London-based front-end web developer with seven years of experience. I design and build responsive, high-performance websites, data-visualisations, interactives and web-apps.

My core toolset includes HTML, Sass, ES6, D3, Yeoman, Grunt, Git, WordPress and Photoshop.

I can write solid and well-organized code from scratch, and I have a passion for exploring new and emerging web technologies. I ensure that my projects are optimised, responsive, cross-browser compatible and accessible for users and search engines.

Originally from New Zealand, I'm currently living in London and happily employed at DueDil. I also help organise and coach at codebar, a non-profit initiative that aims to help improve diversity in the tech community through weekly programming workshops.

When I'm not designing the web I can be found climbing, skiing, unicycling, and occasionally attempting to juggle.
