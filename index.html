Good design speaks volumes

Hello there!

I'm Richard. I make websites, data-visualisations, interactives and web-apps.

I reckon that if something's worth doing then it's worth doing right. Each of my websites is finely crafted to a high standard. Before a single pixel is published, it must pass an exacting series of tests: First, I polish every design in Photoshop until it shines with an unholy burning light. Then I hand-code the site using only the finest HTML ingredients, sprinkling it with a light garnish of CSS and JavaScript. Finally, each pixel must undergo a gruelling fitness regimen to shave off those unwanted bytes, saving precious seconds of download time. Only then is the site ready to be served piping hot to your browser. Yes, I know that my metaphors don't make sense. Nevermind that.

My primary expertise is in web design, front-end development, web standards, usability, SEO and content editing, and my preferred tools are Photoshop, HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, WordPress and Grunt.

Originally from New Zealand, I'm currently living in London and happily employed at Distilled. When I'm not designing the web I can be found skiing, unicycling, and occasionally attempting to juggle.
